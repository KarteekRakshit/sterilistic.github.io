Project ARA

The event is based on Google’s Project ARA. It tests your analytical, management and marketing skills to build and then sell the ultimate modular ARA phone. You and your team are the creators.

Date: 31st March 2015

Venue: Tech Park

  • Round 1
  • Round 2
  • Round 3

  • This will be a filtering round.

  • Teams will attempt a general tech/Google products Quiz.

  • 10-12 Teams will qualify.

  • Coordinators' or the judges' decision is final

  • This round will focus on making the phone

  • This round will be played in the form of an auction. Teams will be buying modules for their ARA phones from us which we will sell to them at prices depending on the configuration of the module.

  • The teams will have to fill their ENDO with 7 modules purchased from the auction.

  • Every team’s task is to buy themselves one of EACH of the modules (such as RAM, Battery, Camera etc.) within the Amount of money given to each team.

Format of Auction
  1. The Auction will be held in rounds. The no of rounds will be equal to the no of teams participating.

  2. In Each round, we will auction ONE of each of the modules (RAM, Battery, Camera etc.) of a certain configuration. Teams will NOT know what the configuration of the same module in the next round will be. It is purely a matter of chance.

  3. Say for example, in Round 1, a team buys 1 GB Ram Module for 5000, and in Round 2, we sell 4GB Ram which another team was able to buy in 6000, it is a loss for the previous team.

  4. The total Selling Price of the phone will be equal to the Sum of Cost of Each Module.

  • This round will focus on selling the phone

  • The Teams will now be provided stationary using which they will have to design Posters, Banners etc highlighting the features of their ARA and also set up the stalls in a Trade Fair fashion.

  • The teams will then have to Market their product to judges who will be given Tokens/Votes and will vote the best phone based on how it is marketed to them and also on the features of the phone.

  • The team with the maximum number of votes wins.
